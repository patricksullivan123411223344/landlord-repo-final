# PROVIDENCE RENTERS ASSISTANCE MASTER KNOWLEDGE BASE

## 1. LEGAL RIGHTS (RHODE ISLAND LAW)
- **Security Deposits:** Cannot exceed 1 month's rent. Must be returned within 20 days of move-out.
- **Entry:** Landlords must provide 48 hours (2 days) notice before entering, except for emergencies.
- **Evictions:** A "5-Day Demand Notice" is required for non-payment before a landlord can file in court.
- **Lead Paint:** For buildings built before 1978, landlords MUST provide a Lead-Safe Certificate.

## 2. PROVIDENCE CITY SERVICES & UTILITIES
- **Electricity & Gas:** Handled by Rhode Island Energy (1-800-322-3223).
- **Water:** Providence Water (401-521-6300).
- **Trash Pickup:** Handled by the City. Bulk items require a scheduled pickup via 311.
- **Snow Parking:** When a "Snow Emergency" is declared, street parking is banned. Use city-designated lots (available on the ProvidenceRI.gov website).

## 3. NEIGHBORHOOD PROFILES
- **Federal Hill:** Vibrant, parking is difficult, high student/young professional population.
- **East Side/College Hill:** Historic, high rent, strictly enforced parking.
- **Elmhurst/Smith Hill:** Residential, popular for students at PC and RIC.
- **Olneyville:** Growing arts district, more affordable but varying building quality.

## 4. EMERGENCY & DISPUTES
- **No Heat/Water:** This is a code violation. If the landlord doesn't fix it in 24 hours, contact Providence DIS at 401-680-5203.
- **Legal Aid:** Contact Rhode Island Legal Services (RILS) for low-income tenant support.
- **General Help:** Dial 2-1-1 (United Way RI).
