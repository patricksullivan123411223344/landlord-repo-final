# Rhode Island Repairs and Habitability Notes

Tenants often ask about repairs, heat, utilities, and habitability standards in Rhode Island leases.

Best-practice workflow for a tenant:
1. Report repair issues in writing with dates and photos.
2. Keep records of all landlord responses or missed responses.
3. Escalate serious health/safety issues through local inspection channels when appropriate.
4. Avoid self-help actions without legal guidance.

Risk signals to highlight:
- Repeated unresolved heat/water outages
- Unsafe electrical or structural conditions
- Retaliatory communication after written complaints

This file is an internal Tenant Shield RAG reference and not legal advice.
